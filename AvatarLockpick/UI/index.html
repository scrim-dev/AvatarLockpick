Help Guide

For an in-depth visual tutorial, be sure to head over to my YouTube channel @ScrimDev!

Step 1: Preparation

Launch VRChat and switch into the avatar you wish to unlock. Wait for the avatar to load completely (I recommend waiting a few extra seconds to ensure it is fully cached). Then, enter your VRChat User ID and the Avatar's ID into the application.

Step 2: Unlocking

Attempt to unlock the avatar using the standard Unlock method. If the avatar utilizes custom or VRCFury locks, use Unlock (VRCFury) or Database Unlock instead.

Finally: Restart

Restart your game using the restart buttons within the app. Enjoy your newly unlocked avatar!
